## Overview

New healthcare providers joining the FoldHealth platform faced a grueling 12-step onboarding process. Compliance requirements, credential verification, and system setup created a gauntlet that caused 40% of providers to abandon registration.

## The Challenge

Healthcare onboarding is inherently complex — there are legal, regulatory, and technical requirements that can't be skipped. The challenge wasn't to remove steps, but to make the experience feel effortless despite the underlying complexity.

## Research & Analysis

Mapped the entire onboarding funnel and identified where drop-offs occurred:
- Step 3 (credential upload): 15% drop-off — unclear file requirements
- Step 7 (schedule setup): 12% drop-off — overwhelming calendar interface
- Step 9 (compliance forms): 8% drop-off — legal jargon fatigue

## Design Strategy

### Smart Grouping
Consolidated 12 steps into 5 logical phases: Profile, Credentials, Schedule, Preferences, Review. Each phase groups related tasks that the provider can complete in one sitting.

### Progressive Completion
Providers can save progress and return later. A persistent progress indicator shows completion status, reducing anxiety about the process length.

### Inline Guidance
Replaced separate help docs with contextual tooltips and examples. For credential uploads, added visual guides showing exactly what document format is needed.

### Pre-fill Intelligence
Integrated with NPI (National Provider Identifier) databases to auto-fill known provider information, eliminating redundant data entry.

## Results

- Onboarding drop-off reduced by **60%**
- Average completion time down from **45 minutes to 18 minutes**
- Provider satisfaction with onboarding: **4.6/5**
- Support tickets related to onboarding decreased by **70%**
