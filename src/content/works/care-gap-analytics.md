## Overview

Care gaps — missed screenings, overdue follow-ups, unfilled prescriptions — cost the healthcare system billions and put patients at risk. I designed a tool that surfaces these gaps to providers at the right moment, in the right context.

## The Challenge

Providers were drowning in data but starving for insights. Existing reports were PDF-based, generated monthly, and required cross-referencing multiple systems. By the time a care gap was identified, the window to act had often passed.

## Research

Shadowed 8 clinical staff members across 3 facilities. Mapped their daily workflows and identified intervention points where care gap data would be most actionable.

Key insight: providers don't need more dashboards — they need the right data surfaced within their existing workflow.

## Design Approach

### Contextual Alerts
Instead of a separate analytics dashboard, care gap alerts appear within the patient chart during appointments. The provider sees relevant gaps at the moment they can act on them.

### Risk Stratification
Not all care gaps are equal. Built a visual risk scoring system that helps providers prioritize: which patients need attention first?

### Batch Actions
For population health managers, designed a batch view that allows filtering, sorting, and bulk outreach for patient cohorts with similar care gaps.

## Results

- Care gap closure rate improved by **28%**
- Provider time-to-action reduced from **days to minutes**
- Adopted across **12 clinical facilities**
- Featured in FoldHealth's annual product keynote
