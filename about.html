    I am an UX Designer that specialises in creating human-centered digital experiences. I love to bring the simplest usability experience using minimalistic design choices.

    About Me

    Everyday I’m motivated by the opportunity to solve real problems for people. I’m always trying to improve my craft, continue learning, and help others grow as designers.
    I am a Computer Science Engineering Graduate who accidentally found a field that combined my interests and my hobby. I love bringing the right mix of design to the field of engineering.

    My Experiencee

    Dec 2017 – Mar 2018

    Graphic Designer Intern at HaappyApp, Bengaluru

    During my first year in college, I got this internship offer in a new start-up. I worked on the Branding of the company along with creating designs for the Social Media and Marketing team. Also helped in the design of the website of the company. I learnt a lot about working as a team and meeting deadlines.


    Sep 2018 – Nov 2018

    UI Designer at Doortask, Bengaluru

    After my internship period was over, they offered me a contract role. Over the course of this contract, I helped on 2 projects, a new app and an overhaul of their existing website. I created wireframes and helped create a simplified set-up & flow of the software application. I contributed to the visual design of the app, along with the prototype and define the interaction models and motion behaviours for the app. I also contributed to UI animations and helped the developer with everything possible.


    Nov 2018 – May 2020

    UI/UX Designer at Doortask, Bengaluru

    I worked on the UI Design as well as the front-end development of their website. I contributed towards the features and user flows of their website as well as prototyping. Also worked on overall visual design and Branding.


    Oct 2020 – Aug 2021

    User Interface Designer at Sentient Studio, Bengaluru

    As the only UI Designer in the team, I was solely responsible for all Web & App Design Projects. I created user-flows, wireframes, design systems and high-fidelity mockups for the User Interface. I was also involved in the front-end development of most of the projects I was involved in. I also developed a few WordPress sites for the clients.


    Aug 2021 – Present

    User Experience Designer at Banyan Cloud, Bengaluru

    Working on a brand new enterprise product, trying to bring consumer level accessibility to an enterprise product, keeping the right balance between functionality and ease of use.
